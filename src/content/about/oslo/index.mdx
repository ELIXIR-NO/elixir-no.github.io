The ELIXIR@UiO node is situated at the [Department of Biosciences](https://www.mn.uio.no/ibv/english/) at the [University of Oslo (UiO)](https://www.uio.no/english/studies/study-at-uio/index.html), 
where it is part of the [Bioinformatics in Life Science (BiLS)](https://www.mn.uio.no/bils/english/) community. 
In autumn 2026, ELIXIR@UiO and BiLS will relocate to the [Life Science Building](https://www.uio.no/english/research/strategic-research-areas/life-science/about/building/).

ELIXIR@UiO offers services related to data management and analysis to researchers in the life sciences. We have a strong focus on bioinformatics and sensitive data within health, especially in cancer research. Among our tasks is the development of solutions for handling and analysing sensitive -omics data,  also to support molecular precision diagnostics. 
Recently, ELIXIR@UiO has also taken a leading role in biodiversity data.

The ELIXIR@UiO node collaborates closely with the [Oslo University Hospital](https://www.oslo-universitetssykehus.no/oslo-university-hospital) and its 
[Bioinformatics Core Facility (BCF)](https://www.med.uio.no/english/research/core-facilities/bioinformatics-core-facility/). 
ELIXIR@UiO and BCF@OUS jointly operate UiO's [Bioinformatics Core Facility/ELIXIR helpdesk](https://www.med.uio.no/english/research/core-facilities/bioinformatics-core-facility/).

You can meet the ELIXIR Oslo team every second Tuesday at 10, when we attend BiLS Seminars. BCF@OUS hosts a research support drop-in session every Wednesday at 14:30 on Zoom.
